Dual-Layer Forensic Watermarking:

  • Layer 1Random Position Markers: Semi-transparent binary markers at pseudo-random positions each frame. Hard to crop or blur out since position varies.

  • Anti-tampering: Random positions per frame make removal extremely difficult
